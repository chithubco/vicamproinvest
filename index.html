What is VIP

Vicampro Investment Plan (VIP) is an innovative product from Vicampro Farms LTD (VFL). The objective of the project is to create a vehicle for individuals who want to participate in agriculture but do not have the time to do so directly. VIP allows investors to generate passive income by investing in the cultivation of high-value commodity crops like maize and soya-beans. Investments are broken down into lots which is equivalent to a hectare of farmland. Investors are guaranteed a 30% return on their investment over a 12-month period. The investor provides the financial investment to develop the field. VFL as the operator is responsible for delivery of the yield from the lot. We encourage everyone to invest in this innovate model for organic growth in the agricultural industry. The model provides a win-win situation for all stakeholder (investor, operating company, communities, economy).

Frequently asked questions

We would love to clarify some of the pressing questions you might have?

What is the guarantee that I would get my money back?

The project will be insured by Leadway Assurance which covers the entire activities of the project. Vicampro has provided insurance cover for all existing farm projects, so that in the event of unforeseen circumstances, the sponsor's capital can be refunded. The insurance covers only the initial sponsorship capital; it does not cover the return after harvest.

What is the minimum investment ?

The minimum investment is one LOT which is equivalent to ₦550,000 ( Five hundred and fifty thousand naira only)

What is the maximum investment size

For the 2018 growing season the maximum investment size is 1,000 lots which is equivalent to ₦550,000,000

How safe are the operational areas in the light of current happening

The farm sites are located in areas not prone to crisis. VIP is implemented on the Kaduna field located in Manchok on the highly busy Kaduna-Jos-Abuja expressway. This area has never witnessed any form of crisis in the past and there is a strong presence of the relevant security agencies who give security cover to our investments in this region.
