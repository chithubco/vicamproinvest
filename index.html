What is VIP

Vicampro Investment Plan (VIP) is an innovative product from Vicampro Farms LTD (VFL). The objective of the project is to create a vehicle for individuals who want to participate in agriculture but do not have the time to do so directly. VIP allows investors to generate passive income by investing in the cultivation of high-value commodity crops like maize and soya-beans. Investments are broken down into lots which is equivalent to a hectare of farmland. Investors are guaranteed a 30% return on their investment over a 12-month period. The investor provides the financial investment to develop the field. VFL as the operator is responsible for delivery of the yield from the lot. We encourage everyone to invest in this innovate model for organic growth in the agricultural industry. The model provides a win-win situation for all stakeholder (investor, operating company, communities, economy).
